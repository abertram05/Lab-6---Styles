Beginner Crochet Projects

Here are some beginner crochet projects that are very simple, and can help you practice the basics of crocheting.

Projects

  1. Dishcloth

    This is the perfect beginner project to practice stitches, while crocheting rows of either single or double crochet, until it is a square.

    • Skills: Chains, Single or Double Crochet Stitch, Turning Chains
    • Materials: Yarn of your choice, a hook, scissors, needle, and stitch markers if needed

  2. Granny Square

    This is one of the most basic beginner projects, that a lot of people start out on. You can make one square for a coaster, or make a bunch of squares and join them together to make so many projects.

    • Skills: Chains, Double Crochet, Slip Stitch
    • Materials: Yarn of your choice, a hook, scissors, needle, and stitch markers if needed

  3. Blanket

    Some may think a blanket isn't a beginner project, but if you make an entire single crochet or double crochet blanket, it is the perfect way to start practicing stitches.

    • Skills: Chains, Single Crochet (Double or Half Double. Choose any stitch you want to practice!), Slip knot.
    • Materials: Yarn of your choice, a hook, scissors, needle, and stitch markers if needed
